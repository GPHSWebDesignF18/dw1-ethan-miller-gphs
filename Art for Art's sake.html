Art for Art's sake

Dates running:
January 1 - June 30
Mon - Fri: 12:00 PM to 6:00 PM
Sat - Sun: 12:00 PM to 5:00 PM
Art for Art's sake is an exhibit opening in the Seattle Art Museum simply about the appreciation of the works being shown.
Some examples of the works on display are "American Gothic" by Grant Wood, "Mona Lisa" by Leonardo Da Vinci, and "Starry Night" by Vincent Van Gogh.
Come to the Seattle Art Museum to see these wonderful masterpieces in Art for Art's Sake!
