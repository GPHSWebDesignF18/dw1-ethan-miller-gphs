Captain America: Backstory and Other Information

Captain America, or Steve Rogers, was born on the first of July, in 1918. He first appeared in the Captain America Comics, starting in March of 1941. The character was created by cartoonists Joe Simon and Jack Kirby. He has also made appearances in The Avengers, other comic series and more. Captain America was once a frail, fragile young man who was enhanced to peak human perfection through a serum known as Super Soldier serum. He aided in the Axis Powers' war efforts, but was frozen in ice late in the war and survived to present day, when he was revived.



Captain America: The History of His Comics

Captain America made his appearance in his very own comic series for the first time ever in March of 1941. The first edition of "Captain America Comics" was released, and put on the shelves of comic book stores everywhere. The public warmed up to him quickly, and the series quickly became one of the most widely read comics in the "Golden Age" of comic books. First editions of this series can now sell for up to 1,500 dollars!

Captain America: Shield Almighty

Unarguably one of the most notable parts of Captain America is his signature shield, bearing red, white and blue stripes with one large,whiteish silver star in the center. This shield acts like a boomerang, it always comes back to Cap. The shield has been seen blocking bullets, knocking down bad guys and even being used as a boost to help colleagues and teammates of Captain get some air. The shield is impenetrable, and undefeatable. Undeniably the best companion to carry with you on the battlefield.

Captain America: An Avenging Appearance

Captain America makes one of his most famous appearances in the movie series "The Avengers", including films such as "The Avengers: Age of Ultron", "The Avengers: Infinity wars"(parts one and two) and more and was even given his very own film series. He is also known as the first avenger.
